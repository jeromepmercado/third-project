ARCS

HASHIRAS

The nine most formidable and proficient swordsmen in the Demon Slayer Corps, the Hashira are the highest-ranking members under the Corps leader. They are regarded as the "pillars" of the organization, each with a special Breathing Style and incredible abilities to fight demons.

UPPERMOONS

The Twelve Kizuki, a group of formidable demons that serve Muzan Kibutsuji, the Demon King, include the six greatest demons known as the Upper Moons. Upper Moon One is the strongest on a ranking of one to six. The Demon Slayer Corps, who must vanquish an Upper Moon in order to become a Hashira, frequently causes the members of the Upper Moons to evolve throughout time.

KAMABOKO SQUAD

Tanjiro Kamado, Zenitsu Agatsuma, Inosuke Hashibira, and Tanjiro's demon sister, Nezuko Kamado, make up the primary squad of young demon slayers in the anime and manga Demon Slayer, which fans called the Kamaboko Squad. The name originates from Inosuke mispronouncing Tanjiro's name as "Gonpachiro Kamaboko," a food product. While initially referring to the main trio, the term is sometimes expanded in fan discussions to include other characters like Kanao Tsuyuri and Genya Shinazugawa.
